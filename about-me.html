About me

Hello!

My name is Francesca Lupu, i am 24 years old and I live in Limerick, Ireland.
I was born in Romania but I grew up in Italy.

I graduated in "International management" specialized on hospitality at the NHL Stenden University in the Netherlands and I did my internship in Brussels, Belgium.
Last year I decided to move to Ireland and I worked as Front Office Agent in a hotel till the start of the pandemic.

During all these months that kept us home I decided I needed a change and that I wanted to create myself a new career.
That's why I started studying to become a Full Stack Developer.

If you ask my previous employers or professors they will tell you that I am a creative person, that I am able to work in teams, that I am flexible and I easily adapt to new circumstances.
I consider myself a fast learner and an enthusiastic person, capable to take the best from every situation.
My background taught me to deal with customers and provide them with the best solutions to their needs.

At the moment I am looking for a job as a Junior Front End Developer to start this new career.
If you are an employer and have a vacant position in your company or if you would like to know more about me, please, do not hesitate to click on the "contact" tab and send me a message.
I will look forward to work together!
